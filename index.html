About the aloft data

Pressure-level winds and cloud cover are model-level data, not observations. Cloud cover at pressure levels is approximated from relative humidity. The winds-aloft page also shows current/modelled sea-level pressure and the forecast sea-level pressure for the selected aloft time. The 100 m / 250 m / 500 m wind views interpolate between the available pressure levels; the rain nowcast does not interpolate on this page and only shows returned 15-minute precipitation steps.
